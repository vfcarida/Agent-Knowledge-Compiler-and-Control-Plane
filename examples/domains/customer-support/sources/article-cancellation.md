# Account Cancellation and Data Retention Policy

## 1. Standard Cancellations (Self-Service)

Customers on Standard and Premium tiers should be directed to cancel their subscriptions via their Account Settings page. Agents should avoid manually cancelling subscriptions unless the customer is physically unable to access their account due to a technical error.

## 2. Enterprise Cancellations

Enterprise customers cannot cancel their contracts via self-service. Any request to cancel an Enterprise contract must be escalated immediately to the dedicated Account Manager.

## 3. Data Deletion (GDPR / CCPA)

When a customer requests the permanent deletion of their account and all associated data ("Right to be Forgotten"):

- **Standard/Premium:** Agents can initiate the deletion workflow. Provide the customer with the `macro-data-deletion-warning` which explains that this action is irreversible and they will lose all data.
- **Enterprise:** Deletion requests from Enterprise accounts must ALWAYS be escalated to the Legal team and Account Manager. Enterprise contracts often have complex data retention requirements or ongoing audits that prevent immediate deletion.

## 4. Retention Periods

If a customer simply cancels their subscription (without requesting data deletion), their data is retained for 3 years in a dormant state before automatic purging.
